100% Grass Fed Burgers

Burger Craft is the ultimate Craft-Your-Own hamburger experience in The Colony, TX. Unlike common fast food places, we want you to Craft-Your-Own hamburger where you can choose either a “bun” or “bowl” from locally sourced, humanely raised and produced products. From our beef, to our produce, to our eco-friendly containers, Burger Craft is sustainable, local, humane and making conscious choices whenever and wherever possible. Burger Craft caters to the lifestyles of those who place importance on a menu that is committed to clean local farm fresh food, when making a dining decision.

Our menu places exceptionally good flavor combinations such as our signature BC Burger, a 100% grass-fed beef hamburger with cheddar cheese, caramelized onions and our special BC sauce within your reach. Another fan favorite is the BLTE Burger, which is topped with our smokehouse bacon, a fried egg, white american cheese with a maple syrup drizzle – something you can’t get from a typical fast food joint! You can also come get your mac on with some macaroni and cheese or thrill your taste buds with an all natural hot dog. Complete your meal with a handmade milkshake or our fried cheesecake.

One thing is certain – you can feel good about making conscious choices without sacrificing taste! Come visit us today!

Gourmet Hamburgers

Burger Craft takes pride in the careful handling of every single meal they make and aim to be more than a normal fast food restaurant. From locally sourcing only the best organic ingredients, to preparing and finally serving their food they are intimately involved in making sure every meal they serve is the best you have ever tasted.

Healthy Food

By only using 100% grass fed beef and sourcing organic ingredients Burger Craft is able to deliver healthier, better tasting fast food! Compared to their competition, Burger Craft meals have 80% less cholesterol, 30% less fat and 50% more Omega3 making them the smart choice!

Vegeterian Friendly

If you follow a strict diet or hamburgers aren’t really your thing, Burger Craft may still have something just right for you. The Burger Craft menu offers a variety of options including gluten free, kosher, vegetarian and vegan menu items. Don’t let your diet keep you away and come visit them in The Colony today!
